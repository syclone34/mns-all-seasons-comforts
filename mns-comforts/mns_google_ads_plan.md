## Conversion Tracking — What to Set Up

1. **Phone Call from Ad** — Google auto-tracks this when you enable Call Extensions
2. **Phone Call from Website** — Install Google Tag on the site, track clicks on the `tel:7632911615` link
3. **Form Submission** — Track the "Thank You" state shown after the contact form submits

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Without conversion tracking, Google cannot optimize your bids. Set this up on Day 1 before turning campaigns on.
